URJC SMART ACCOUNT

URJC SMART ACCOUNT

Welcome to the future of finance with our URJC Smart Account! It's not just a bank account; it's your passport to a world of limitless possibilities.
With innovative features and an unwavering commitment to your financial success, our account is the vehicle that will take you to your biggest dreams. Feel the excitement of a bright financial future and join us on this journey to greatness. Your ambition deserves the best bank account, and that's exactly what we offer - discover now the features that make it so special!

Send and receive money

Need to pay a friend back in advance, split the lunch or contribute to a birthday gift? No problem! With our money transfer feature, you can do it quickly and hassle-free.
Receiving money is just as easy - simply share your account information with friends, family or customers, and receive payments securely and hassle-free!

Profile customization

From changing your profile picture to selecting a color theme that suits your taste, our customization tool allows you to create an online space that is completely your own. Do you like elegant simplicity or do you prefer something more vibrant and colorful? You decide!

Apply for loans

Reach your financial goals faster with our "Loan Request" feature! With just a few clicks on your mobile device, you can access a variety of loan options designed to meet your specific needs.

Account analytics

With interactive graphs and informative charts, our analysis tool gives you a bird's eye view of your income, expenses and trends over time. Want to know how much you spend on groceries each month? Or how much you've saved in the last six months? Our analysis feature tells you everything clearly and accurately!

Notification system

Receive instant alerts about transactions, account balances and important activities directly to your mobile device or via email. Customize your notification preferences to fit your lifestyle and financial priorities, making sure you never miss anything important.

Smart expense filtering

You can set custom rules and criteria to organize your spending into relevant categories, allowing you to automate the process and save time in managing your finances. Want to receive alerts when you spend more than usual in a specific category? Our intelligent filtering system lets you do just that!
